Making a tomato mixed Egg
  1. Take an egg
  2. Break the egg and put it in a bowl
  3. Take onions and chop it into small pieces
  4. Now, Take tomatoes and chop into small pieces
  5. Put choped onions and tomatoes into the bowl where eag was kept
  6. Mix them well
  7. Heat a pan
  8. Add some oil on the pan and let the oil be warm
  9. After 1 min put the mixed egg into the pan
  10. After some time flip the egg
  11. Keep it in a plate
  12. Serve it
